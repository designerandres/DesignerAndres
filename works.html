Works:
I'm a passionate, dedicated graphic designer. I consider myself a devout artist & go-getter. I'm that sidewalk-doodler, that classroom-hand-raiser, that 5-am-designer, that stubborn re-trier. I'm that "darer," that rule-bender, that word-maker-upper, that "darned dreamer". I'm inspired by rule-bending dreamers.

I specialize in motion graphics, print, front-end web development, and UI/UX design. I've lived in Caracas, Orlando, and most recently New York City, and in my many travels I've gained a love and appreciation for the art of design and its impact on culture and business. I've worked in Graphic Design, UI, and Photography at The Walt Disney Co., INROADS Inc, AAA National, and Social Learning Project, NY.

Below I share a few samples of my identity, layout and composite designs, as well as a video reel of motion graphic clips I've worked on over the course of the past several years. In addition, this portfolio website is a showcase of my front-end development work. From wireframing, to responsive concepting, ux flow, and into hardcoded, semantic HTML5, CSS and Javascript, this site represents a major portion of my late-night coding. Luckily, this has brought me and Coffee closer together over time. We couldn't be happier.

MESSAGING
When we design furniture we design it for the humans using it. We need to shape a chair after the human body. When we design print or digital products, we design it for the humans reading them. The science that helps us making sure that people can and will read those publications is typography. It's the craft of making visual communication readable, legible and comprehensible. If it's done well it will go even farther and make communication pleasant. Typography makes content palatable for the human brain. - Killian Muster→
BRANDING
Logo design is one of many services that a good graphic designer can provide to a professional company that is looking to make an impression on the marketplace. With the absence of a memorable, eye catching and meaningful logo design, a business - no matter how small or large - will fight an uphill battle when it comes to name and brand recognition. With great logo design, a company's name will resonate clearly with all of their customers.
VISION
Call it vision, imagination, or seeing; it all comes down to the same thing: the ability to envision a final result in your mind's eye, and then to make it so with your tools at hand. It's never been about the gear. It's always been about seeing something, knowing how you want it to look, and making it so. Making it so is the easy part; seeing it in the first place is what makes a photographer. Powers of observation are everything. - Ken Rockwell→
